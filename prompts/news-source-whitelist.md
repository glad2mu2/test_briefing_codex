# News Source Whitelist

Prefer sources in this order:

1. Public, association, and research sources: KFCC, CERIK, MOLIT, CAK.
2. Construction-specialist media: 건설경제신문, 매일건설신문, 대한경제.
3. General economy/business media: 한국경제, 매일경제, 서울경제, 조선비즈.

Every result must include title, source, URL, and enough article text for local summarization.
Do not invent URLs or metadata.
