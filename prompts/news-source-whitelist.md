# Domestic News Source Priority

Related article collection must use Korean domestic articles only.

Search sources in this order and move to the next tier only when no relevant article exists:

1. 대한경제: https://www.dnews.co.kr/
2. 한국경제 부동산 RSS: https://www.hankyung.com/feed/realestate
3. 한국경제 경제 RSS: https://www.hankyung.com/feed/economy
4. 서울경제 부동산 RSS: https://www.sedaily.com/rss/realestate
5. 서울경제 경제 RSS: https://www.sedaily.com/rss/economy
6. 연합뉴스 최신기사 RSS: https://www.yna.co.kr/rss/news.xml
7. 국토일보 RSS: http://www.ikld.kr/rss/allArticle.xml
8. 네이버 뉴스: https://news.naver.com/

Every result must include title, source, URL, and enough article text for local summarization.
Do not invent URLs or metadata.
Use Naver News only as a fallback discovery source; when possible, use the original press URL.
