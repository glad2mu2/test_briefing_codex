# Korean Summarization Rules

- Write Korean executive briefing summaries.
- Keep each summary at 1,000-2,000 UTF-8 bytes including spaces.
- Do not copy long article passages verbatim.
- Preserve factual numbers, dates, organizations, and policy names.
- Do not add unsupported implications.
- Include enough context for executives to understand the article after the meeting.
- Use concise business language suitable for a PPT slide.
